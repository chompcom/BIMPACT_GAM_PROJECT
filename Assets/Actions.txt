ApplySlowToTarget
	applies slow speed modifier

MoveToTarget
	move to target
SafeDistancePlayer
	keep away from player at safeRadius
TargetPlayer
	set target to player
TargetRandomEnemy
	set target to a random enemy in list
TargetEnemyInDetectionRadius
	find the closest enemy in detectionRadius and target them
Wander
	activate wander timer and move in a random direction
TargetMiddle
	target the middle thing
TargetCorner
	move to the nearest corner if not already in corner
ShootProjectile
	shoots a projectile at the rate given by attackRate
CircleMove
	move in a circle around target
DVDMove
	move like the dvd logo (bounce around)
DVDBounce
	do a bounce "off" the target
BecomeAngry
	change state to angry
BecomeNeutral
	change state to neutral
DamageTarget
	straight up damage the target at an attack rate. Be careful with this one.
PullTarget
	"pull" target towards self like a gravity. Strength based on strength.
PushTarget
	"push" target away from self. Strength based on strength.

AttackAnim
